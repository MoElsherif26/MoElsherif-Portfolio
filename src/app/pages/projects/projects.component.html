LearnHub

Key features include parental involvement, allowing parents to monitor their childrens progress and view grades.
The platform also includes a robust question bank where instructors can add questions, enabling the system to generate unique quizzes for each student based on configurable parameters while ensuring consistent difficulty levels.
The system provides automatic quiz correction upon submission or time expiration, supporting both multiple-choice and essay questions in English and Arabic.
